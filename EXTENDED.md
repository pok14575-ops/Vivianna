## Extended Explanation

The top part of the readme should already reveal a lot of what happened, but this might help anybody stepping onto this trail I followed. This trail was trailblazed by people before me and most gave up or didn't publish their results. This extended explanation will contain the story, timeline and detailed insight of the design choices made.

---

### How I fell into the rabbit hole

It all started with the enthusiast self assembled gaming rig ([Lian Li O11 Evo build](https://www.reddit.com/r/lianli/comments/1miav2e/1575_build_lian_li_o11_evo/)) in autumn 2025. The specs are overkill for most games, therefore I had the urge to make it actually work instead of being partially unemployed. This was the point where I remembered AI exist and image generation was the easiest documented entry. Within 40 days I went from Stable Diffusion over A1111 to ComfyUI. I encountered terminology I had to google or even GPT explain to me. The first encounter with an LLM happened shortly before I mastered Inpaint and Outpaint, in form of Encoder to Prompt. Then a second time in voice cloning and a third time in video creation.

---

### How is that related to this project?

This project is a mix of my transferred think pattern, which emerged after exposure to frontier LLMs. I started to notice patterns which I was not able to perceive before the ComfyUI days. It felt like crossing a threshold and I could not look away anymore. What if I could generate more than images or text with more control like with ComfyUI? By transferring my shallow knowledge of what ComfyUI does (basically the same using python just with a UI), I outpainted the plan from the model choice to the playback of TTS. Inpaint helped me to refine the orchestration and latency masking, and the corresponding detailer pass translated to doctrine. Every thought train finished led to more questions and answers, more terminology, more insights. I saw frontier models no longer as a single multi-B parameter answer directly. I realized they are huge clusters, a datacenter orchestrating my input to output via delegation. Not one big super brain, instead a lot of agents, gates, judges and specialized smaller models for the exact context and task.

---

### Chatbot to Vision

Vivianna started as a simple primitive chatbot like all others. I wanted more than that by any means. The hardware constraints limit my options, but they do not limit my creativity. This meant it is impossible to copy a datacenter structure and reasoning power, but I can at least get close to the orchestration. By shrinking the scope and size of what the frontier does, I would be able to achieve the closest to it locally. This led me to the point where I entirely fell into the rabbit hole. It was the point where I imagined her future and what she shall become at the end. Can you imagine a nanny for a household? One that is able to terminate a YT window or application after three warnings followed by an explanation about its content to my kids? No API keys, no monthly subscription, as local as possible. We can't beat the frontier in terms of reasoning power and scope, but we can reach for sovereignty and orchestration.

---

### Design Choices made

Exactly how every beginner does start — it's the model. Qwen is the only model family that has the capabilities I am looking for. Language strong on native Chinese for grandparents, English for me and development, and decent German for the household. The most important point speaking for Qwen was another: its open weights and public availability in continuous releases. Modularity is one of the major aspects of this project. To withstand time and aging, it needs to be able to adapt to circumstances yet unknown to me. Anything that breaks the project can be replaced or redesigned. Every leap in hardware or releases will only make Vivianna become better in one way or another. The doctrine holds together the architecture and design. The hardware constraints influence the doctrine and that one translates to design again. A trifecta which is only possible by having a clear vision of what I wish Vivianna to become — a continuous, stable and warmer nanny than any RLHF-heavy frontier model can be, due to Consumer Policy, ToS and Regulations.

---

### Why perception over benchmark?

This is the point where the concept becomes clear completely. Benchmarks cannot measure my use case specifically. I can't do empirical tests like researchers — it would break this hobby in scope and time. So my personal best outcome is no delay perceived by any guest or household member from end to end. None of them are developers nor close to tech in general. With enough masking of the hidden processes running in the background, they will perceive realistic warm responses. Example given: this prototype is able to explain Quantum Field Theory in its simplest form with analogies to the family, like sitting at the dinner table, with no perceivable delay — achieved by immediate acknowledgement of the task given, followed by an apology for the wait time ahead, into a detailed analogy explanation. The LLM is done long before audio playback ends. Though a nanny does not need to do such a task, nor should she for future homework of kids, so she can refuse those politely — which means reasoning power is not the focus.

---

### Giving back by leaving a trail

I came to the point I am today not on my own. There have been many before me and many have given up on fully local on Windows especially. This is me, giving anybody willing to follow this trailblazed path a hint, a bit of guidance and a point they could start their journey from. I started with nothing, I do not end it here. It is the point where I am glad that I was able to give something back that may help anybody start from where I came from.
